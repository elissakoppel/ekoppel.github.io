Warning

Though I am made of atoms small and few I am massive in my scale and in my strength. I can kill in a myriad of ways.

      In a tsunami, I kill through force, crushing those who fall under my waves hundreds of feet tall.
      In a flood, I kill through suffocation. I fill the lungs and esophagi of creatures, preventing oxygen from doing its work.
      In a drought, I kill through absence.
